Injuries

  • Patriots edge rusher Harold Landry placed on physically unable to perform list

    Harold Landry has been placed on the physically unable to perform (PUP) list as he continues to recover from a knee injury that limited his performance last season. The absence of Landry, who led the Patriots in sacks last year, further depletes the team's edge rusher depth, especially with fellow second-round pick Gabe Jacas still unsigned and facing complications from surgically repaired injuries. This move places additional pressure on young players like Elijah Ponder and Bradyn Swinson to step up in Landry's absence.

Contracts & Business

  • Christian Gonzalez’s extension talks loom over Patriots’ training camp preparation

    Christian Gonzalez, a key cornerback for the New England Patriots, has been eligible for a contract extension for some time, but negotiations have yet to be concluded. As discussions around a potential extension heat up, Gonzalez, who is positioned to reset the cornerback market, has been less active in team practices, raising concerns about how the contract situation could affect his performance throughout the season. The outcome of these talks could have significant implications for the team's salary cap and future roster decisions.
